Phases & Deliverables
Phase 1
POLISH
Apr 13 – Apr 26 · Weeks 1–2

Objective. Refine core positioning, narrative and GTM messaging to clearly articulate value to partners and funders.

Key Deliverables

  • Pitch v1 draft — donor + investor variants (Apr 22)
  • Objection map covering ≥10 objections
  • Expert feedback session with ≥3 reviewers
  • Curated 10-org target list with pursuit rationale
Phase 2
PRACTICE
Apr 27 – May 10 · Weeks 3–4

Objective. Pressure-test pitch with a friendly but credible audience to improve clarity, flow, and donor/investor resonance.

Key Deliverables

  • 2 virtual practice pitches, ≥4 participants each
  • Feedback Log entries for every participant
  • Pitch v3 finalized by May 13
  • Stretch: ≥1 participant → warm intro for Phase 3
Phase 3
PITCH
May 4 – May 24 · Weeks 4–6

Objective. Take refined pitch into the market and initiate real conversations with qualified partners, donors, and investors.

Key Deliverables

  • ≥3 live pitches to qualified, named prospects
  • All pitches logged in Feedback Log + Partnership Matrix
  • Written follow-up within 48 hours per pitch
  • ≥1 prospect → defined next step (LOI / pilot / grant)
Phase 4
ASSESS
Late May – End of June

Objective. Capture learnings and hand P3 a forward plan with a validated path to revenue and capital development.

Key Deliverables

  • Written end-of-engagement summary
  • 90-day roadshow & roadmap plan
  • Extension decision (yes / no / defer)
